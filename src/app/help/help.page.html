How to Play

Endless game

With this app you can play against a computer and check if you are smart in picking the right attributes. The score is counted and this is the endless mode.

Team Game

The cards are devided into 2 teams. You want to get all the cards from your oponent. By chosing the right attribute you can win a card of you oponent!

Available attributes are:

1. Year of Birth: The older person wins

2. Power: The general power of this person according to Teemu and Andi. Higher power wins

3. Speed: The speed of the person according to Teemu and Andi. Faster speed wins

4. Wealth: The higher the value wins

Keep in mind that all the values are not actually telling anything about the mentioned persons.
